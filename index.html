Overview: Apple Business Manager Integration

To distribute our iOS application securely and in compliance with Apple's latest App Store requirements, client organizations must access private business applications through Apple Business Manager (ABM).

To enable access, your company must register for a free Apple Business Manager account and share your Organization ID with us.

Why is this required?

Based on Apple's app review determination, our application was rejected for public App Store distribution because it is classified as a B2B application for specific client organizations, not a public consumer app.

Why Apple rejected public listing:
  • • Manual Onboarding: Client companies are manually onboarded by our team.
  • • No Self-Creation: Engineers cannot create their own accounts (accounts are created by the client company).
  • • Exclusive Audience: Only employees of client companies can use the app.
  • • No Public Functionality: A random person downloading the app cannot use it.

To Apple, these parameters mean the app is intended for a limited set of organizations, which falls under App Store Review Guideline 3.2 – Business. Consequently, private distribution via Apple Business Manager is the only permitted distribution path.

Your Apple Business Manager administrator generates free Redemption Links inside the portal and distributes them directly to your engineers (via email or chat).

Engineers click their individual redemption link on their iOS device, which opens the App Store to download and install the app.

They then log in using the exact same credentials already provided by your company.

No changes are required to engineer accounts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions regarding enterprise Apple distribution.

Is Apple Business Manager free?
Yes. Completely free. There are no registration fees, monthly recurring costs, or per-seat charges assessed by Apple for setting up and utilizing Apple Business Manager.
Do we need to purchase any Apple subscription?
No. You do not need to enroll in the Apple Developer Program ($99/yr) or purchase any Apple enterprise subscriptions. Apple Business Manager is a free standalone portal for end-user organizations.
Do we need Mobile Device Management (MDM)?
No. MDM is not required. Apple Business Manager works perfectly without an MDM solution. Your administrator can distribute the application using free Redemption Links generated directly in the portal. While you can connect an MDM if your company uses one, it is completely optional.
Will engineers need new login credentials?
No. Engineers will continue using the exact same login credentials already provided by your organization for our service. No changes or resets are required for engineer user accounts.
