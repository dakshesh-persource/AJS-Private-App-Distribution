What is Apple Business Manager?

Apple's official web-based portal for organizations to securely manage apps and device ecosystems.

Completely Free

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions regarding enterprise Apple distribution.

Is Apple Business Manager free?
Yes. Completely free. There are no registration fees, monthly recurring costs, or per-seat charges assessed by Apple for setting up and utilizing Apple Business Manager.
Do we need to purchase any Apple subscription?
No. You do not need to enroll in the Apple Developer Program ($99/yr) or purchase any Apple enterprise subscriptions. Apple Business Manager is a free standalone portal for end-user organizations.
Do we need Mobile Device Management (MDM)?
No. MDM is completely optional. Apple Business Manager works perfectly without an MDM solution. While you can connect an MDM if your company uses one (such as Jamf, Intune, or Kandji), it is not a requirement to receive and distribute our application.
Will engineers need new login credentials?
No. Engineers will continue using the exact same login credentials already provided by your organization for our service. No changes or resets are required for engineer user accounts.
Will this affect Android users?
No. This requirement applies exclusively to the iOS application distributed through Apple's ecosystem. Android users and distribution remain completely unaffected.
